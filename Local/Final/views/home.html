AES Encryption Explorer

Welcome to AES Encryption Explorer, an interactive educational website designed to deepen your understanding of Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) —the gold standard for secure data encryption. Whether you're a student, researcher, or cybersecurity enthusiast, our platform provides an engaging way to explore AES through live demonstrations and performance-optimized hardware acceleration.

Features

  • Live AES Encryption Demo - Encrypt and decrypt messages in real-time using our intuitive interface.
  • Hardware-Accelerated Performance - Experience AES encryption offloaded to a PYNQ FPGA board for optimized speed and efficiency.
  • Timing Optimization Analysis - Compare software and hardware execution times to see the power of acceleration in action.
  • History & Evolution of AES - Learn about the origins of AES, its adoption as the U.S. government encryption standard, and why it remains crucial for cybersecurity today.
